Master's student

College of Control Science and Engineering, Zhejiang University


I am a master's student advised by Professor Yu Zhang, at College of Control Science and Engineering, Zhejiang University.

My research interests lie in visual SLAM in degraded environments, thermal SLAM, and LiDAR-visual-inertial odometry. Currently, I am making thermal cameras working in challenging environments, such as poor illumination and smoke. I am also making efforts to implement a robust SLAM system by fusing LiDAR, thermal, and inertial sensors.
